Army - Solider Technical

Indian Army releases Clerk Bharti Rally notification for different states and regions. The details are as follows:

Educational Qualification : 10+2/Intermediate examination passed in Science with Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and English with 50% aggregate and 40% in each subject.

Age Limit : 17½ years to 23 years

Selection Process :

Individuals who are able to qualify with their required eligibility criteria are then selected for further selection process. During the selection process the applicants will be participating in different tests.

Each of these test is based upon particular factor, as a matter of fact this stage consist s Indian Army GD physical fitness test, intelligence ability test, medical test.

Stage-1 Physical Fitness Test
Stage-2 Written Exam

1- Physical Fitness Test
This test is commonly known as the Army Soldier GD physical fitness test. Individuals who have been selected for this test are required to complete the following tasks.

1.6 Km Running : Qualifying
Pull Ups : Qualifying
Zig-Zag Balance : Qualifying
Nine Fit Ditch Jump : Qualifying

Stage 2 – Written Exam
Individuals who pass the fitness test are then called for the written exam which will be an intelligence ability test. Selection of the candidates in this test is determined by their score.

The written exam is based upon the exam pattern and syllabus that has been selected for Indian Army Soldier GD written exam as provided below:

Exam Pattern:

Subject Ques Marks Pass Marks
General Knowledge 10 20 40
Maths 15 30 40
Physics 25 30 40
Chemistry 10 20 40
